Urban Exploring During COVID-19

During this pandemic, I’ve spent more time on taking walks, and exploring the place I call home.

What I’ve found out, is that there are so many sacred places that I overlook every day. Places and structures that have been totally abandoned, with their history barely remaining.

A friend and I have combined our photos to present to those interested, the forgotten spots of Kansas City. Stay tuned!
